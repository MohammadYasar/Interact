Interact: A Large-scale Multimodal Dataset

Paper | Website | Documentation

Overview

Interact is a large-scale multimodal dataset comprising human-human and human-robot collaboration data. It features tasks, variations to investigate obstacles' impact on human motion, 3-D skeleton data, RGB+D data from two viewpoints, and ego-view of two participants. The dataset is large-scale with 252 episodes.

Comprises Human-Human Collaboration (3 Humans) data on a simulated assembly task. Link
